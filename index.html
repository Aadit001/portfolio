Case Studies

BT Group

Reducing "Where is my order?" queries by 34%

Problem

BT's UK digital marketplace was flooded with post-order support tickets. "Where is my order?" was the #1 query category, driving up contact centre costs and eroding customer satisfaction (low Transactional NPS).

Approach

Mapped the end-to-end post-purchase journey, identified gaps in proactive communication, and designed self-service order tracking workflows with real-time status updates. Ran iterative usability tests and worked with engineering to integrate carrier APIs.

Outcome

Support queries reduced by 34%, Transactional NPS improved by 2 points, and the post-purchase cross-sell strategy is projected to drive £5M in incremental digital revenue.


Aakash

Automated Lead Scoring to improve conversion by 8%

Problem

Sales reps at Aakash were wasting effort on low-intent leads. The CRM had no intelligent prioritisation, leading to poor lead-to-conversion rates and missed revenue targets.

Approach

Conceptualised a Lead Scoring model within Salesforce using behavioural signals (page visits, content downloads, demo requests). Collaborated with data engineering to build scoring rules, validated with historical conversion data, and iterated thresholds with sales leadership.

Outcome

Lead-to-conversion rates improved by 8%, agent productivity increased by 55% through streamlined workflows, and the CRM platform scaled to support 5,000+ users.
